The Red Cliffs Desert Reserve is a protected natural area designed to conserve desert wildlife and stunning landscapes. It features dramatic red rock canyons, rare desert tortoises, and scenic hiking trails just minutes from St. George.

Red Cliffs is a peaceful spot for hiking and enjoying Utah's unique desert wildlife.
