Popular Restaurants

  • Cliffside Restaurant: Has beautiful views of the city and temple.
  • Benja's Thai and Sushi: My personal favorite restaurant in St. George. I even used to work there!
  • Angelica's Mexican Food: The best Mexican food in town!
  • Durangos: A more casual Mexican restaurant like Cafe Rio, but much better! Get the sweet pork salad!

Local Favorites

While St. George doesn't necessarily have a specific dish or type of food that originated here, the Mexican and Thai food are by far the most popular in town. My family's favorite restaurants are Benja's Thai and Sushi and Angelica's Mexican Food. Every time I go to visit, we end up eating at one of these two spots.
